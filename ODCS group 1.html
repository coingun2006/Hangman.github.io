What is hangman?

History of hangman?

It is thought that Hangman originated in the 19th century, while its exact roots are unknown. The game's essential idea hasn't changed throughout time, despite versions going from pencil-and-paper to digital.

Objective of the game hangman.

In a set amount of tries, the object of Hangman is to guess a secret word by recommending letters. The player's probability of losing the game increases with each wrong guess; this is commonly symbolized by a stick figure being "hanged."

Rules of the game?

Configuration:

A single player pens down a string of blank spaces for each letter in a word or phrase that comes to mind.One letter at a time, the other player makes guesses in an effort to guess the word.

Playing the game:

The letter is revealed in the relevant blank space(s) for every accurate guess.

A portion of the hangman figure (usually the head, body, arms, and legs) is drawn for every wrong guess.

Gaining and Losing:

When the guessing player correctly guesses the word before the hangman is completely drawn, they win.
If the hangman is finished before the word is guessed, the player who came up with the word wins.

In summary

Hangman is a straightforward but captivating game that enhances vocabulary and critical thinking abilities. Played in a more competitive environment or just for fun with friends, it's still a well-liked past time that can be had anytime, anywhere.
